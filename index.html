About This Game

Brief Introduction

This is a simple RPG game with a twisted story — you’re the princess going through a challenging but rewarding journey to find your fiancé whom is hidden by your wicked Uncle to prevent you getting married with him. To save your fiancé, you will enter your Uncle’s dark world and fight you way to your love while encountering thousands dark soldiers. You can avoid them or kill them, your game your choice!
